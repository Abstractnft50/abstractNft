About

Abstract NFT is a limited edition collection of digital art created from elements of design and ideas with endless limits and posibilites. By owning a Abstract NFT you will be an exclusive member who will be eligible for giveaways, voting rights for suggesting new collection themes, future airdrops, collabaration and many suprise. Abstract NFT is build on an idea of making something possible that has been one's dream "Owning a NFT collection". Absract NFT creates artwork with unexplored themes. What was just an idea in mind is a vision for us to build. Through the Abstract NFT initiative, we hope to bring NFT one step closer to collectors by bringing the art to the world through the blockchain path.
